Wes Crues

Hey! I'm R. Wes Crues. I am wrapping up a PhD at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in Educational Psychology. I studied how we can use text mining and natural language processing to glean insight into the replicability and reproducibility crisis facing the social sciences. I've also worked a bit in understanding how students behave in online learning environments (e.g., MOOCs, online classes) via clickstream data.

NarOpt is a project I built as an Insight Data Science Fellow. I'm in the remote program.
